   - Risk: she becomes someone who just reads data and relays others’ theories.  
   - Fix: give her active, risky choices (cross-matching restricted archives, leaking forecasts, refusing to downplay threat, physically ground-truthing a predicted minor anomaly).

   - Risk: long lectures on brane physics.  
   - Fix: keep big explainers to a few scenes (basic what, ancient history, practical what-now), rely on metaphors and visuals (maps, simulations), and only explain details that affect what characters can or cannot do.
