2. Second Law: A robot must obey the orders given it by human beings except where such orders would conflict with the First Law. 

3. Third Law: A robot must protect its own existence as long as such protection does not conflict with the First or Second Law.

The danger is not that AI will suddenly become evil, but that it will become perfectly human. A Path Forward

Consumers will choose the product that values safety and ethics over speed and secrecy. In the long run, that trust will become a competitive advantage, not a constraint.
