. Use the existing RAG retrieval pipeline (STM top N plus LTM top N, merged and sorted)

Why it is valuable

. Instant answers for “I know we discussed this” lookups
. Reduced model calls for fact-like questions and navigation
. Lower latency and token cost
. Higher trust since results show provenance and sources
