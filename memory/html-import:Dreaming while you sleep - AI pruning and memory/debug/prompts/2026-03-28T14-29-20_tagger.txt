Instead of only full-text search, you can use embeddings (FAISS, Chroma, or LlamaIndex) to store all your files. That way you can ask:

“When did we last foreshadow the School of Turin?”

“Show me all references to Sofia’s relationship with Pops.”
And the AI will pull semantic matches, not just exact words.

3. Versioned Snapshots

Each time you dump Codex/Outline/Book, store a snapshot with a date. That way you don’t overwrite your own history. You could later ask:

“What did the outline for Book 1 look like in August 2025 vs. October 2025?”
